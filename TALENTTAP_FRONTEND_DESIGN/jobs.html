Senior Frontend Developer

TechCorp Inc.
View Profile
Full Time Remote $100k - $130k 5+ years experience

We are looking for an experienced Frontend Developer to join our team. You will be responsible for building our user interface components, integrating with our backend services, and ensuring a smooth user experience across all devices.

Responsibilities:
  • Develop new user-facing features using React.js
  • Build reusable components and libraries for future use
  • Translate designs and wireframes into high-quality code
  • Optimize components for maximum performance across devices
  • Collaborate with the backend team to integrate user-facing elements with server-side logic
  • Stay up-to-date with emerging trends in frontend development
Requirements:
  • 5+ years of experience in frontend development
  • Strong proficiency in JavaScript, CSS, HTML
  • Experience with React.js and its core principles
  • Experience with popular React workflows (Redux, Hooks)
  • Familiarity with RESTful APIs
  • Good understanding of responsive design and cross-browser compatibility
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail
Benefits:
  • Competitive salary and equity package
  • Flexible working hours and remote work options
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance
  • 401(k) plan with company match
  • Professional development budget
  • Paid time off and parental leave
  • Home office stipend
About TechCorp Inc.

TechCorp is a leading technology company focused on developing innovative solutions for the financial services industry.
