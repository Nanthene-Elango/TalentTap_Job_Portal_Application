Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I post a new job?

To post a new job:

  1. Click the "Post a Job" button in the top navigation bar
  2. Fill out the job details form with title, description, requirements, etc.
  3. Set the compensation range and job type (full-time, part-time, etc.)
  4. Review and click "Publish Job"

Your job will be live immediately and visible to potential candidates. You can edit or pause the job posting at any time from the "Manage Jobs" section.

How do I review and manage job applicants?

You can review and manage applicants in two ways:

  1. From your dashboard, click on "Recent Applicants" and then "View All Candidates"
  2. Go to the "Candidates" tab in the main navigation

There, you can filter candidates by job position, view resumes, update their status (New, Shortlisted, Interview, etc.), and contact them directly through our messaging system.

How do I upgrade my subscription plan?

To upgrade your subscription:

  1. Click on your profile in the top-right corner
  2. Select "Subscription" from the dropdown menu
  3. Review the available plans and their features
  4. Select your desired plan and follow the payment instructions

Your account will be upgraded immediately upon successful payment. If you have any billing questions, please contact our support team.

How do I update my company profile?

Keeping your company profile updated is important to attract quality candidates:

  1. Click on your profile in the top-right corner
  2. Select "Company Profile" from the dropdown menu
  3. Update your company information, logo, description, and culture details
  4. Add company photos and videos to showcase your workplace
  5. Save your changes

A complete company profile increases your visibility and helps candidates understand if they'd be a good fit for your organization.

How do I generate and export reports?

To generate and export recruitment reports:

  1. Navigate to the "Reports" section in the main navigation
  2. Select the type of report you want to generate (applicant sources, job performance, etc.)
  3. Set the date range and any other filters
  4. Click "Generate Report"
  5. Use the "Export" button to download as PDF, Excel, or CSV

These reports can help you optimize your recruiting strategy and track your hiring metrics over time.

How to add team members to my account?

To add team members who can help manage your recruiting process:

  1. Click on your profile and select "Account Settings"
  2. Navigate to the "Team Members" tab
  3. Click "Add Team Member"
  4. Enter their email address and select their permission level (Admin, Recruiter, Viewer)
  5. Send the invitation

Note: The number of team members you can add depends on your subscription plan. Premium and Enterprise plans allow for unlimited team members.
